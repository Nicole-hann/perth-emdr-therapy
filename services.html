EMDR & Trauma-Informed Therapy

Eye Movement Desensitisation and Reprocessing (EMDR)

I am fully trained in EMDR and advanced EMDR, qualified to perform EMDR, which is currently one of the leading evidence-based treatments for adverse life events, PTSD, C-PTSD, childhood trauma, negative or stuck beliefs, OCD, Anxiety, Phobias and many other conditions.

For standard EMDR please allow a minimum of 10 sessions. This includes assessment, identifying targets/goals, increasing your resources, performing eye movements, installation and enough time to debrief and close EMDR smoothly.

To learn more about EMDR or to book an appointment for EMDR please go to www.perthemdr.com.au
