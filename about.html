About Nicole

Endorsed Counselling Psychologist with a passion for EMDR and trauma-informed care.

A warm and welcoming approach to healing

Nicole is an endorsed Counselling Psychologist who has been in private practice for over 10 years. With a special interest in EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitisation and Reprocessing) and trauma-informed interventions, she brings both expertise and genuine warmth to every therapeutic relationship.

Nicole founded Perth EMDR Therapy with a vision to create a warm, welcoming, and professional space where clients feel safe to begin their healing journey. She is currently building a team of like-minded therapists and Psychologists who share this commitment to high-quality, evidence-based care.

Perth EMDR Therapy is actively recruiting qualified Psychologists and EMDR therapists who align with our values of compassion, professionalism, and client-centred practice.

All of our therapists are fully trained and qualified in EMDR, and we work closely with each client to create a treatment plan that feels right for them — whether that's telehealth, in-person, or an at-home service.
